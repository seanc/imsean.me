This is a compilation of my previous work with clients.

Rusher’s Discord

Rusher’s Discord is an application that allowed users in TheCampingRusher’s Discord server to purchase subscriptions and receive their roles in games for various rewards and giveaways. I worked with PoleStar design who designed the brand and website.

The application required Steam and Discord integration along with PayPal for the billing gateway and a Discord bot to automatically assign the roles. Additionally a small administration panel was required to display basic analytics about daily, weekly, and monthly profits as well as users and their subscription status.

Sean created an amazing website for TheCampingRushers Discord. We gave him a layout that we had designed and a lowdown of what we wanted it to do. His communication was everything we wanted from a developer. Quick, informative & helpful. We would be more than willing to work with Sean again.

– Curtis (TheCampingRushers Manager)

BetaSniper

I developed a website for BetaNyan’s client sniper with the Bulma CSS framework on top of AdonisJS. The application included a license registration, validation and management system along with a basic CMS to upload and manage new releases.
